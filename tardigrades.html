Tardigrades

Tardigrades are considered to be the toughest animals on Earth. This toughness has allowed them to survice on Earth for over 600 million years. To give you a better idea of what that actually means lets convert years to seconds and look at some ages.

600 Million seconds is just over 19 years. Modern humans evolved in Africa 200,000 years ago, so to a 19 year old Tardigrade we evolved just under 2 days and 8 hours ago. We started settlements and stopped wandering around eating bugs about 3 hours and 20 minutes ago, the Pyramids were built an hour and a quater ago and we've had electricity for about 2 and a half minutes

Tardigrades have survived all 5 mass extinction events. The most successful evolutionary period so far was the Jurrasic age that saw the evolution of all the most famous dinosaurs right up to their extinction. For a 19 year old Tardigarde that would have been from roughly their 13th birthday to under a year and a half later. Many scientists believe that Tardigrades are tough enough that they will stay alive on Earth until the Sun burns out in 5 Billion years and even then they will probably hold out a while. At 19 Tardigrades are probably babies on their way to being over 160 years old.
